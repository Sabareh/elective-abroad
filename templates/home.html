Why Choose Elective Abroad?

Elective Abroad is the leading organizer of Healthcare internships and volunteer placements in Africa. No one comes close to the quality and responsiveness of our programs; we organize an end-to end placement and mean every word that you read.

Our experience in organizing pre-medical hospital exposure, medical Nursing, & Dental electives gives you an experience of a lifetime! Get hosted by native experts who are there from the pre-departure process till airport drop-off when you bid us bye.

Program Pricing

At Elective Abroad, we prioritize affordability because we understand that pursuing your dreams shouldn't break the bank. Our pricing structure is designed to be competitive, ensuring that you receive exceptional value for your investment in your education and international elective program.

Transparency is at the core of our pricing philosophy. We believe that you should always know exactly what you're paying for, and that's why we have a commitment to no hidden fees. When you choose Elective Abroad, you can trust that the price you see is the price you pay.

What Our Participants Say

Cassanne Eccleston, Charles Sturt University Australia

"Doing a medical elective in Kenya was an eye opening experience. I got to see the developing world health system and range of tropical diseases not normally prevalent in Australia. I got to learn and perform skills like administering injections, catheters and suturing patients. I was also able to take part in the examining, differentials, diagnosing and formulating treatment plans. The highlight for me would have to be the amazing new friends I have made and the motivation I have taken back home. I would recommend this program to anyone considering it."

Ida W. Bjorntvedt , Oslo , Norway

"Elective Abroad offers more than just medical placements. It's a cultural adventure that enhances your perspective. I volunteered in Tanzania and had the chance to explore the local culture and traditions. The team's commitment to making the experience unforgettable is truly commendable."

Dr. David Johnson

"As a volunteer doctor, I was impressed by the facilities and the level of support provided by Elective Abroad. My time in Zambia allowed me to make a meaningful impact on the local community. The program is well-structured and enriching for medical professionals."
